About Me

Hello and welcome to my site! My name is Justin, and I've been playing video games since I was a kid. I got my start in the early 90's playing PC games such as "Resident Evil" and other various junk ware my Uncle had. This eventually led to console gaming with the Game Boy, Nintendo 64 and others. My favorite console is the Nintendo 64, I LOVE "Pokémon Stadium" and Pokémon games in general.
Thanks for visiting and I hope you enjoy my site!
