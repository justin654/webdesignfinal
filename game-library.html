  • Pokémon Crystal Japanese

    Released: December 14, 2000

    System: Game Boy Color

    Explore Pokémon Crystal, the Japanese version of the beloved classic. Journey through Johto, capture legendary beasts, and experience unique cultural nuances. Uncover the Celebi event, engage in epic battles, and relive the magic of this timeless adventure in its original form. Whether you're a seasoned trainer or a newcomer, Pokémon Crystal Japanese Version offers an unforgettable experience filled with nostalgia and excitement.

  • Pokémon Stadium

    Released: April 30, 1999

    System: Nintendo 64

    Step into the thrilling world of Pokémon Stadium, where epic battles between your favorite Pokémon trainers unfold in glorious 3D. This classic Nintendo 64 game allows you to transfer your trained Pokémon from your Game Boy cartridges and witness them in stunning detail as they duke it out in the Stadium. With a variety of challenging modes, including Gym Leader Castle and mini-games, Pokémon Stadium offers hours of fun and strategy for trainers of all ages. Test your skills, assemble your dream team, and become a Pokémon champion in this iconic and unforgettable gaming experience!

  • Tetris

  • Glover

    Released: October 26, 1998

    System: Nintendo 64

    Join Glover, the enchanting magical glove, on an adventure for the Nintendo 64! In this platformer, you'll guide Glover through a vibrant and imaginative world, solving puzzles, battling quirky enemies, and utilizing your unique ability to manipulate magical balls to progress. With captivating gameplay, charming graphics, and a dash of humor, Glover offers a one-of-a-kind gaming experience that will keep you entertained for hours. Dive into this classic N64 title and discover why Glover has remained a beloved and cherished character in the hearts of gamers for generations.

  • Hey You Pikachu!

    Released: October 30, 2000

    System: Nintendo 64

    Forge a unique bond with Pikachu in Hey You, Pikachu! on the Nintendo 64. Speak commands, explore, and play mini-games in this heartwarming interactive adventure. Experience the joy of having Pikachu as your virtual friend and embark on a special Pokémon journey together.
